EyeMind identifies loneliness risk, learns your social preferences, measures engagement through gaze, and offers audio journalling, actionable reminders, PDF sharing, and gamified badges to foster meaningful connections.

  1. Use the AI Risk tab to calculate your loneliness level.
  2. In Gaze Engagement, calibrate and track your attention.
  3. Answer scenarios in DCE to reveal preferences.
  4. View Recommendations, record an Audio Journal, and set Reminders.
  5. Earn Badges and review your Summary. Export as PDF.
