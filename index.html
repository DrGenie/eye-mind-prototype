EyeMind – Hybrid Loneliness Intervention

This prototype brings together AI risk scoring, advanced gaze analytics (with calibration, fixations & heatmap), discrete choice experiments, audio journalling, calendar reminders, PDF sharing, and gamified badges to combat loneliness in older adults.

  1. Navigate to Login and sign in with testuser / welcome123.
  2. Complete the AI Risk questionnaire and view your risk level.
  3. Go to Gaze Engagement: calibrate, track your gaze, view stats & heatmap.
  4. Answer the scenarios in DCE to capture your social preferences.
  5. Visit Recommendations for personalised suggestions, record a short audio journal & set reminders.
  6. Check your earned Badges and review your Summary. Export as PDF or share with caregivers.
